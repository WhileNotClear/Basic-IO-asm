learned:
    rax --- tells the cpu to do something (0 - get input, 1, write, 60 - exit)

    rdi --- file descriptor (0 - input, 1, write)

    rsi --- where to place string or buffer

    rdx --- the len of string or buffer

    syscall --- calls what rax has

    mov --- moves something (mov rsi, msg -> moves msg into rsi)

    .bss --- block starting symbol (const)

    .data --- data (not const)

    .text --- globals

    _start --- where start is or where do your code starts
